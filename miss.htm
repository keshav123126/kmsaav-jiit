MISSION ANTODAYA

Clickable image link to Mission Antyodaya website

1. Objective:
Aims to uplift the most marginalized communities in rural areas by improving their quality of life and economic well-being. Seeks to achieve holistic development through convergence, accountability, and community participation.

2. Scope:
Focuses on critical sectors such as livelihoods, health, education, sanitation, and rural infrastructure. Targets rural poverty alleviation with a time-bound and integrated approach.

3. Convergence:
Combines resources from multiple central, state, and local government schemes for efficient implementation. Encourages inter-departmental collaboration to optimize resource utilization.

4. Mission Antyodaya Survey:
Conducts an annual survey to assess socio-economic conditions and infrastructure gaps in Gram Panchayats. Data-driven insights help prioritize development areas and monitor progress.

5. Community Participation:
Empowers rural communities by involving them in planning, decision-making, and execution. Encourages participatory governance to ensure accountability and inclusivity.

6. Vision:
Strives for sustainable and self-reliant villages by addressing developmental disparities. Aligns with the motto of "Sabka Saath, Sabka Vikas, Sabka Vishwas," ensuring inclusive growth.
